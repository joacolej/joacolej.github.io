About me

I am a PhD student of Pure Mathematics at Sorbonne Université, more specifically at IMJ-PRG under the advisement of Andrés Sambarino. My main field of research is Anosov representations, especially deformations of the Barbot representation in SL(3, ℝ), I am particularly interested in the geometric structures they parametrize. My other interests include hyperbolic geometry, Anosov Representations of Coxeter groups and the pressure metric in the Hitchin component for triangle groups.

If you want to contact me you can find me at lejtreger(at)imj-prg(dot)fr
